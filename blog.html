Bohol Adventure

Bohol is most famous for being the home of the geological wonder called the Chocolate Hills. The Chocolate Hills, which turns into chocolate brown during the dry months, is truly a sight to behold. But aside from these beautiful hills, there are other things that Bohol has to offer..

Bohol Adventure

The Tarsier is a small, nocturnal species of primate that can be found in Southeast Asia. Its best known for its large eyes and prominent ears.

Bohol Adventure

The Bilar Man-Made Forest, also known as the Bohol Mahogany Forest, is an example of a reforestation project gone right. The project, which was started over 50 years ago, is now a tourist attraction in Bohol, and covers over 2 km of land along the border of Loboc and Bilar towns in Bohol.
